Join THUHCSI | 加入实验室

Human-Computer Speech Interaction Lab at Tsinghua University (THUHCSI) welcomes highly motivated students to join our research team.

Our lab conducts cutting-edge research in intelligent speech interaction and multimodal AI, including:

  • Speech synthesis and speech generation (语音合成与语音生成)
  • Speech recognition and spoken language understanding (语音识别与口语语言理解)
  • Audio, music and singing voice generation (音乐与歌声生成)
  • Digital human and audio-driven animation (数字人与语音驱动动画)
  • Multimodal interaction and affective computing (多模态交互与情感计算)
  • Audio security, anti-spoofing and deepfake detection (音频安全与深度伪造检测)

Our research results have been published in leading conferences and journals such as AAAI, NeurIPS, ICLR, CVPR, IJCAI, ICASSP, INTERSPEECH, and IEEE/ACM TASLP. The lab also maintains close collaborations with both academia and industry.

We welcome undergraduate, master’s, and PhD students who are passionate about AI research and interested in speech, audio, and multimodal technologies.

Email Subject Format | 邮件标题格式

Please use the following format for your email subject: (either in English or Chinese)

  • [THUHCSI Application] University – Major – Year – Name
  • [THUHCSI申请] 学校-专业-年级-姓名

Emails without the required subject format may be filtered or delayed.

mail Content | 邮件正文建议包含

Please briefly include the following information in your email:

  • Basic Information: University, Major, Current year or degree
  • Research Interests: Your research interests related to our lab
  • Relevant Background: Courses, Research projects, Publications or competitions (if any)
  • Availability: Expected time commitment, Future study plans (e.g., applying for graduate school)

OR in Chinese:

  • 基本信息: 学校, 专业, 年级或学历
  • 研究兴趣: 感兴趣的实验室研究方向
  • 相关经历: 课程基础, 项目经历, 论文或竞赛
  • 时间安排: 可参与科研的时间, 是否计划申请研究生或博士

Required Attachment | 附件要求

  • Research papers (preferred) or technical reports (论文或技术报告)
  • Portfolio or project description (项目介绍)
